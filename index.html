Get to know Stray Kids a little bit

This K-POP boy group was formed under JYP Entertainment and the group contains eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. It's common that record labels in South Korea to choose every members of a group they want to debut but JYP chose to do something different when forming this group. JYP chose Bang Chan to be the leader of Stray Kids and then let Bang Chan choose the rest of the members he wanted.

The group's name "Stray Kids" was not defined by JYP Entertainment but conceptualized by the members themselves. It originally referred to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and later evolved to represent the idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary.

They are considered a "self-producing" idol group as the members are almost always involved in songwriting and composing, and sometimes assist in arranging. Though the main producers in Stray Kids is Bang Chan, Changbin and Han, and as their own unit they are called 3RACHA.
